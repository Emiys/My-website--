Unspoken

There are day I snap, not because I'm mean, but because something deep inside me feels unheard, unhealed, and heavy. Anger isn't always loud violence. Sometimes it's silent sadness that got tired of being patient.

Learning to let Go

Some days I feel like I'm holding in too much - like if I let it out, I'd fall apart. But letting go doesn't always mean losing. Sometimesit's bravest way to win.

I'm not perfect. I feel deeply. I care loudly. And that's okayyy.

- Me in progress

When Love Feels One-Sided

Sometimes, we give our hearts to someone who doesn't know how to carry them. We wait for a message, a call, a sign... and it doesn't come. It makes us wonder if we're too much - but the truth is, maybe we were just asking the wrong person to see us clearly.

I'm learning that love isn't supposed to feel like begging . That I don't have to shrink myself to fit into someone else's "busy." Real love shows up, even in small ways. And if it doesn't? That's a loud answer

So now I'm pouring back into me. Into my growth, my faith, my passions. Be one day, someone will meet me where I am - and not just when it's convenient.

- Pieces of me, learning to heal
