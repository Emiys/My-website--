When Love Feels One-Sided

Sometimes, we give our hearts to someone who doesn't know how to carry them. We wait for a message, a call, a sign... and it doesn't come. It makes us wonder if we're too much — but the truth is, maybe we were just asking the wrong person to see us clearly.

I'm learning that love isn't supposed to feel like begging. That I don't have to shrink myself to fit into someone else's "busy." Real love shows up, even in small ways. And if it doesn't? That's a loud answer.

So now I'm pouring back into me — into my growth, my faith, my passions. Because one day, someone will meet me where I am — and not just when it's convenient.

- Pieces of me, learning to heal

📝 Emotionally Available but Emotionally Confused 😂💔

July 7, 2025

Sometimes I feel like I'm emotionally available... but also emotionally confused 😭 It's like I want to be there for others, but I also need to figure out my own feelings first.

I think I’ve been doing too much. Like… *tooooo much*. I just want simple vibes, close friendships, and a consistent “wyd” that doesn’t end with “I’m watching a movie.” 😤😭

It’s crazy how I’ll be having a serious moment, trying to talk things out in my head like: “Should I call him or give him space?” …then boom… my brain starts singing childhood theme songs:

✨🎵 “I was a girl in the village doing all rightttt…” 🎵✨

MA’AM. THIS IS A CRISIS. WHY ARE YOU SINGING SOFIA THE FIRST⁉️ 💀

I'm trying to balance everything — faith, friends, family, feelings, school (even though I'm on vacay rn), and lowkey my own brain is not helping 💀

If overthinking was a job? I’d be CEO, CFO, HR, and the intern. I just want real friendships — where we talk things out, vibe, and I don’t have to guess if I’m annoying or you don't like talking to me
