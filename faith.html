God is My Safe Place

When I'm overwhelmed, I go silent - but i whisper to God. I know he hears. He understands what I don't even how to say. He holds the weight I hide from others.

Verses I Hold Close

  • Proverbs 3:5-6- "Trust in the Lord with all your heart,...."
  • Psalm 34:18 - "The Lord is near to the brokenhearted,...."
  • Isaiah 41:10 - "Fear not, for I am with you...."

A Different Kind of Passion

Most kids my age don't care much about God's Word. It's not popular to open a Bible or talk about Jesus. But I feel drawn to Him. I want to know Him deeply - not because I have to, but because I need Him and He loves me. Even when I struggle or drift, something in me always come back to Him.
