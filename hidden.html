🔐 Secret Room

🌌 My Safe Space

This is where I leave my deepest thoughts, prayers, and tears — just between me and God.

🫣 How I Really Feel But Will Deny in Public:

Not gonna lie… I’ve been in my feelings lately 🥲 Not because anyone did anything wrong, but because I care. Maybe too much. Maybe loudly.

I overthink like it's my full-time job. When someone I care about goes quiet, my brain just loads:
💭 “Did I annoy them?”
💭 “Am I too much?”
💭 “Are they talking to someone else who’s funnier or cooler or just… not me?”

I know he’s the kind of person that doesn’t talk a lot. Someone close to him even said texting him is kinda dry — and now I get it. That’s just how he is. And honestly? That’s okay.

I don’t want him to change who he is. I just wish we still had those chill conversations, our Bitmoji twinsies (😭 why did he change his outfit tho), the basketball jokes, the random voice notes.

I’m not even tryna date right now. I just want a strong friendship — the kind where we both feel seen, heard, and safe. You know? Like… real connection.

💔 My Deepest Secret:

Uhm… okay so… this one’s hard to type.

I’m really afraid of losing people. I feel alone in this world a lot, even when I’m surrounded by people. Like I’m not enough. Like no matter how much I try, I’ll always fall short.

I cry a lot. Not for attention. Just quietly. In my room. Hugging a pillow. Telling God things I can't even say out loud.

Sometimes I feel like people are gonna leave… or forget me… or just stop caring.

🍁 Moving to Canada & Missing Home:

Canada is cool. But also… weird. I miss home more than I ever thought I would. I miss my old friends, the vibe, the laughter, the way we understood each other without needing to explain our slang or tone.

I miss shouting “UP NEPA!!!” 😭
I miss being surrounded by people who just… got me.

Here, I feel like I’m always trying to “adjust.” Like my accent makes people pause. Like I have to translate myself — my jokes, my feelings, my whole vibe — just to fit in. And honestly? It’s exhausting.

🪐 Space Mode Activated:

So yeah… I think I need to float in space again. No gravity. No pressure. Just me, God, and whatever healing sounds like these days.

I’m not mad at anyone. I’m just tired of wondering if I’m too much or not enough. I’m learning to pour love back into me. 🌸

Goodnight from me, my overthinking brain, and one dramatic tear gliding down my face like in a telenovela 😭
Catch y’all on the moon. Or maybe Mars.

- Elhan, quietly healing and still hopeful 💜
